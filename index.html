07 — Why This Works
01
Heritage Respected
Orange/amber palette is fully retained. All existing packaging, menus, and signage remain usable. Zero waste, minimal reprint cost.
02
Youth + Premium Balance
Bebas Neue is used by brands like Supreme, H&M, Spotify. Bold enough for youth, refined enough for upscale positioning.
03
Infinite Scalability
The wordmark reads perfectly from a 16px app favicon to a 20-foot restaurant hoarding. The icon works standalone at all sizes.
